About Me

I was born in Tartu and have lived here my whole life. From an young age I have been a really creative kid. Creating art has been my main aspiration and nothing can taken it away from me. In highschool I got to know more about digital art and programming. Since then I have started to create my own little projects. It ranges from digital art to 3-D art and web design. I've always had a strong desire to learn and discover new things, because of this, I am constantly looking for ways to educate myself. I'm a sociable and interactive person who thrives in collaborative environments. I enjoy working with others and believe that the best ideas are born through open dialogue and teamwork. My friendly and kind nature makes me approachable and easy to work with.
